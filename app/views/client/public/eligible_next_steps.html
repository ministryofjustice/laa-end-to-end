What happens next

To decide whether you might qualify for legal aid, we need more information about your financial circumstances.

A caseworker will need contact you to discuss your circumstances and determine your eligibility.

You’ll need to provide evidence of the financial information you’ve given us through this service.

Then they will match you to a legal aid provider who will provide legal help.
